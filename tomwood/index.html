Step Collection

Introducing our new Step collection, a subtile and distinctive mini collection of rings and earpieces. Adding a modern touch, the light gold combined with silver has become a signature detail from Tom Wood since the first signet ring collection. Step is inspired by the iconic mountains in the western part of Norway, such as the iconic Prekestolen, Kjerag and Trolltunga.
